ZENOM SOLAR POWER

With a mission to facilitate the increased development of renewable energy in India through collaboration, education, training, and advocacy.

Vision

For economic and environmental reasons, We believes INDIA, and the Earth will benefit greatly from the rapid development of renewable energy resources and that INDIA can and should be a leader in such development.

To reach that goal, We has adopted five primary objectives:

Promoting energy efficiency.

We are trying to promote energy efficiency by public awareness and camps.

Support renewable energy

With the help of our parteners We Support development of viable renewable energy projects as much as possible.

Policies implementation

Help develop and implement policies that will increase the production of renewable energy in INDIA.

Renewable energy market in india

Build a market in INDIA for renewable energy that Fosters and demonstrates stakeholder unity in support of renewable energy and energy efficiency in INDIA.
